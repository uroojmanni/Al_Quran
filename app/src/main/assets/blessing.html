Surah Yaseen

Introduction: Surah Yaseen is the 36th chapter of the Quran with 83 verses. Known as the "heart of the Quran," it emphasizes the oneness of Allah, prophethood, and the afterlife.

Benefits:
  • Forgiveness of sins.
  • Eases difficulties.
  • Intercedes on the Day of Judgment.
  • Brings blessings and protection.
  • Eases the moment of death.

Surah Al-Mulk

Introduction: Surah Al-Mulk is the 67th chapter with 30 verses. It emphasizes the sovereignty of Allah and the creation of the universe.

Benefits:
  • Protection from the punishment of the grave.
  • Shields from Hellfire.
  • Encourages reflection on creation.
  • Brings nightly protection and blessings.

Surah Al-Kahf

Introduction: Surah Al-Kahf is the 18th chapter with 110 verses. It includes stories of the People of the Cave, Moses and Khidr, and Dhul-Qarnayn.

Benefits:
  • Protection from Dajjal (the Antichrist).
  • Brings light (noor) from one Friday to the next.
  • Provides guidance and wisdom.
  • Offers protection and blessings when recited on Fridays.

Surah Al-Waqi'ah

Introduction: Surah Al-Waqi'ah is the 56th chapter with 96 verses. It describes the events of the Day of Judgment and categorizes people into three groups.

Benefits:
  • Protection from poverty.
  • Brings abundance and sustenance.
  • Provides spiritual insight into the afterlife.
  • Encourages preparation for the Day of Judgment.
